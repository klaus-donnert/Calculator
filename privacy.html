Privacy Policy for Calc 9

Effective Date: March 26, 2025

Overview

At donnert.us, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Calc 9 (the "App") handles information. We're proud to confirm that our App operates without collecting any personal information from users.

Information Collection

Calc 9 does not collect, store, or process any personal data. The App:

  • Operates entirely offline
  • Performs all calculations locally on your device
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers
  • Does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms

Data Storage

All operations occur locally on your device. The App uses:

  • Service Workers: For offline functionality only
  • Cache Storage: To store app assets locally
  • No external databases or cloud storage

Third-Party Services

The App operates independently and does not:

  • Integrate with third-party services
  • Use analytics tools
  • Display advertisements
  • Connect to external APIs

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect:

  • App improvements
  • Legal requirements
  • Security enhancements

Changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above.
